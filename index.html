Short Story

The fog surrounding me was thick, The air was muggy and all sense of the world was gone. As I walked further into the woods it was difficult to know where I was going and when I would get there. Fear was pounding my heart, but there was no going back. I had to keep going. I pushed forward struggling to breathe . The track seemed to be endless. There was no where to go. Lost in the middle of a cold forest with no food, shelter, or heat. That was when I started to panic.
